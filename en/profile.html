My jewels come to life for the sake of beauty. Inside them there is a dialogue between vision, color and the challenge with the potentialities of materials which are - in fact - made precious by the goldsmith art. A love relationship which keeps at a certain critical distance from the object that I am creating and leaves a space where emotion is renewed and an ever new vividness is transferred to such relationship. My most recent works originate from the detail of a photographic shot and they translate the feeling of that particular moment and of that particular shot into a search for color and shapes. Though the finished jewel has to give pleasure to me first of all, it nevertheless represents a dialogue with other sensibilities such pleasure can be shared with. The core of my research resides in my “creating by doing”, by which instants made of lights and shadows get captured, translated, transformed and forged into the shape of a jewel. Each creation has to bear the original and recognizable sign of my artistic style.
